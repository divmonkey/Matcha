Our Philosophy

Tea as a practice,
not a product

Every tin of Matcha Book begins its journey in the mist-covered hills of Uji, where the finest tea farmers have cultivated their craft for over four centuries. We partner directly — no brokers, no compromise.

Single-Origin Guaranteed

Every batch is fully traceable to a single garden. Lot numbers, harvest dates, and farmer profiles in every order.

Stone-Ground to Order

We mill in small batches to preserve volatile aromatics and L-theanine potency. Ground the week you receive it.

USDA Certified Organic

No pesticides, no synthetic fertilizers. The plants shade naturally, deepening their chlorophyll and amino acid content.

Our Story

Born from a pilgrimage
to Uji in 2010

Founder Yuki Tanaka left her career in London to study chado — the Way of Tea — under Grandmaster Hiroshi Okubo. What began as a personal ritual became Matcha Book: an invitation for the world to experience what tea can truly be when it's grown, harvested, and prepared with absolute devotion.
